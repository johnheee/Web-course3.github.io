FAQ:

  • GetHotel.com Rewards

    • To become a Hotels.com™ Rewards member, create an account or sign up on our Hotels.com™ Rewards page. How it works Book a stay at an eligible hotel. Collect 1 Rewards night for each night you stay. Once you have collected 10 rewards nights, you will get 1 rewards night at an eligible hotel or vacation rental. Additional benefits Collect nights for business travel. Enjoy travel perks when you reach Hotels.com® Rewards Silver or Hotels.com® Rewards Gold tier status. Unlock Secret Prices on selected hotels and vacation rentals. Good to know The value of your rewards night is equal to the average rate of the 10 nights you collected to earn it. You can attach a booking to your account that you made prior to joining Hotels.com™ Rewards, if you have not yet completed your stay. For more information, read the Hotels.com Terms and Conditions.

  • Hotels & Vocation Rentals

    • Your upcoming, completed, and canceled hotel and vacation rental bookings are stored in your account. After booking, we send you a confirmation email containing your itinerary. This email is your booking confirmation. To view and email your hotel or vacation rental itinerary Sign in to your bookings page. Select View booking next to the itinerary you would like to view or email. Select Send as email. To find a booking If you don't have a Hotels.com account or can't see your booking listed, you can look up your booking confirmation using your last name and the itinerary or confirmation number emailed with your booking. Still need help? If you can't locate your booking, contact us for help.

  • Flight & Package Booking

    • You cannot view your flight or package bookings in your Hotels.com account. When you book a flight or package, you will receive a confirmation email that includes: Your itinerary information Your Hotels.com itinerary number Your 6-character flight confirmation code You will not receive a paper confirmation, receipt, or tickets. We recommend printing out your itinerary and confirmation code before traveling. To receive an email copy of your package or flight itinerary Go to Find your booking. Select Request confirmation email. Enter the email address associated with your booking, and select Send. Still need help? If you can't locate your booking, contact us for help.
